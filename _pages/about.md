The ThingSet prototocol was mainly developed as a flexible and easy-to-use method for device configuration and monitoring via CAN bus (especially for the Libre Solar MPPT and BMS devices). The protocol ideas were found to be useful also for other devices and other low-layer protocols with low data rates such as LoRa. Thus, the specification was generalized and both binary and text-based versions were combined to one standard.

The protocol specification is published under the [Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike License](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/), so it completely open for both commercial and personal use.

ThingSet is currently still under development. You can participate via Github.

After the specification is finalized, implementations for Arduino and other platforms will be published.
